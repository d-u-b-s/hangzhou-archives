Black Diamond Conversions is an ongoing project of rendering ideas and iconography in a modular motif designed specifically for tattooing.

Each pattern is composed exclusively of black trapezoids. As tattoos age, the ink spreads slightly through the skin, and the lines become less defined. Normally considered undesirable, the Black Diamond motif anticipates and incorporates this natural degradation. As the tattoo ages on the skin, the small breaks between the lines start to connect, and the tattoo becomes whole as it is “grown into.”
