eggshells, paint, digital collage on aluminum print
(installation digitally rendered),

A literal interpretation of the phrase, "walking on eggshells," this project attempts to highlight the complexity of navigating conversation in 2020 with over 500 individual shells, each of which displays a word or phrase of possible contention. An image of each individual shell is presented as part of the installation to allow viewers to break the shells without limiting the content for others.
