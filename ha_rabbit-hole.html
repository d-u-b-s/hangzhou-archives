A “rabbit hole” is a figurative expression for a bizarre or arduous situation from which it is difficult to extricate oneself. In this literal interpretation of a contemporary rabbit hole, a compilation of 10,000 flagged facebook memes fall from the ceiling.

This project investigates the meme as a new source of communication, humor, prejudice, contention, and indoctrination.
