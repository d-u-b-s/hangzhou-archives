The motivation for my work is derived from a sincere concern for the world I live in. I find value in testing, and sometimes infringing, the limits of our collective sensibilities because it helps clarify what these sensibilities are and whether they are worth reconsidering. By continuing to investigate these limits, I hope to discover new means of relief that might help us deal with living in the current moment.
