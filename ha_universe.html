High-resolution scans of inkbrush paintings on archival paper.
(max res. 7500px per painting)

Physicists have come up with a number of theories about the end of the universe and given them fun names like the “Big Crunch, Big Freeze, Big Rip, etc. This project attempts to derive beauty from the cosmic process of a universe coming to its conclusion with 137 paintings in ink depicting different iterations of the same event. Each painting commemorates 100,000,000 years our own universe has been in existence.
